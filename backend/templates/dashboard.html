MT5 EA Setup
  1. Download gold_trading_ea.mq5 from the release package.
  2. Copy it to your MT5 MQL5\Experts\ folder and compile (F7).
  3. Open an XAUUSD chart on M5 timeframe.
  4. Drag the EA onto the chart. In Inputs, set:
    ApiKey = {{ user.api_key }}
    SignalUrl = {{ config.BASE_URL }}/api/signal
  5. Check Allow live trading and Allow WebRequests.
  6. Enable Auto Trading in the MT5 toolbar.
